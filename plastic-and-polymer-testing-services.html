Standards and Compliance

Our testing laboratories operate under rigorous British and European standards, ensuring comprehensive material validation across all industry sectors. We maintain full compliance with the BS EN ISO framework, including critical standards such as BS EN ISO 527 for tensile properties, BS EN ISO 179 for impact testing, and BS EN ISO 1133 for melt flow characteristics. In the post-Brexit landscape, our dual certification approach ensures materials meet both UK REACH and EU REACH requirements, facilitating seamless trade across European markets. For medical applications, we adhere to the stringent requirements of BS EN ISO 10993 and MHRA guidelines, guaranteeing biocompatibility and safety for healthcare materials. Construction sector testing follows the UK Construction Products Regulation (UK CPR) and relevant British Standards, while our packaging analysis meets the comprehensive requirements of EU No 10/2011 for food contact materials. Our UKAS accreditation and compliance with ISO/IEC 17025 demonstrates our commitment to testing excellence. We maintain active participation in international standards development, including collaboration with ASTM International and alignment with global testing protocols. This comprehensive approach ensures that materials tested in our facilities meet current requirements while anticipating future regulatory developments, particularly in areas of sustainability and circular economy principles. Emerging standards in microplastics testing, biodegradable polymers, and recycled content verification form an integral part of our testing portfolio, supporting industries in meeting evolving environmental regulations. Our validation processes include rigorous method verification, uncertainty measurements, and regular proficiency testing, providing clients with reliable, internationally recognized results that support global market access.

Key Criteria for Testing

Our advanced testing methodologies deliver thorough analysis across five critical dimensions of polymer performance:

  • At the foundation of our testing regime lies mechanical property analysis, where we conduct sophisticated evaluations of material strength characteristics. This encompasses precise measurements of tensile strength using advanced dynamometers, multi-point impact resistance testing, detailed flexibility assessments, and comprehensive hardness testing across multiple scales.
  • Thermal characterisation forms another cornerstone of our analysis, employing state-of-the-art calorimetry and thermal analysis equipment. We evaluate critical parameters including heat deflection temperature, precise melting point determination, advanced flammability testing to international standards, and thermal conductivity for insulation applications.
  • Our chemical resistance testing utilizes advanced spectrometric and chromatographic techniques to assess material stability. We examine polymer responses to diverse chemical environments, from aggressive solvents to environmental stressors, providing detailed degradation analysis and lifetime predictions.
  • Durability assessment involves sophisticated accelerated aging protocols, incorporating advanced UV stability chambers and weather simulation equipment. Our comprehensive fatigue testing regimes evaluate long-term performance under real-world conditions, while environmental exposure testing assesses material endurance across diverse climatic conditions.
  • In response to growing environmental concerns, our sustainability analysis provides detailed assessments of material biodegradability and recyclability potential. We employ advanced composting simulation equipment and recycling compatibility testing to evaluate environmental impact throughout the product lifecycle.

Comprehensive Plastics & Polymer Testing
1. Packaging Materials

We test materials like bottles, containers, films, and bags used in food packaging, industrial wrapping, and sustainable packaging. Our tests focus on strength, sealing, and compliance with food safety regulations to ensure your products meet consumer expectations and industry standards.

2. Automotive Components

Plastics play a key role in automotive design, from bumpers to dashboards and door panels. Our testing ensures that automotive components are lightweight for fuel efficiency, durable for long-lasting performance, and capable of withstanding the harsh conditions of vehicle operation.

3. Electrical & Electronics Components

For connectors, housings, and circuit boards, we assess properties such as electrical insulation, heat resistance, and flame retardancy. This ensures that materials used in electrical and electronic components can operate safely under extreme conditions while preventing potential hazards.

4. Medical Devices & Equipment

Testing medical plastics such as syringes, IV tubes, and catheters is critical to ensure biocompatibility, resistance to sterilization processes, and overall patient safety. We provide tests that comply with the stringent regulations required in the medical industry.

5. Construction Materials

From pipes to insulation and window frames, plastics are essential in modern construction. Our testing services help verify the strength, durability, and energy efficiency of construction materials, ensuring they meet building standards for plumbing, weatherproofing, and insulation.

6. Consumer Goods

Plastics are commonly used in toys, furniture, and kitchenware, and we conduct safety tests to ensure these products are child-friendly, durable, and compliant with safety regulations. Whether it’s strength, wear resistance, or overall quality, we ensure your consumer goods meet expectations.

7. Textiles & Fibers

For textiles such as clothing, upholstery, and carpets, our testing focuses on strength, wear resistance, and flame retardancy. This ensures long-lasting performance, comfort, and safety in daily use, particularly in products requiring high durability or resistance to fire.

8. 3D Printing Materials

We offer testing for 3D printing filaments like PLA, ABS, and PETG to ensure optimal performance for prototyping, industrial production, and custom manufacturing. Our tests evaluate material strength, flexibility, and heat resistance, crucial for the successful creation of 3D printed parts.

9. Adhesives & Sealants

Plastics used in adhesives and sealants (such as glue, caulking, and silicone) undergo testing to ensure strong bonding, waterproofing, and long-term performance. This testing supports their use in construction, automotive, and manufacturing applications.

10. Sporting Goods

Plastics are used in a variety of sporting goods, including tennis rackets, helmets, and athletic shoes. Our tests ensure that these materials are lightweight, durable, and capable of withstanding high-impact performance during use.

11. Film & Sheet Materials

We test materials like stretch film, shrink wrap, and plastic sheets used in packaging and protective coverings. Our services ensure that these materials are durable, provide proper protection, and meet the needs of industrial applications.

12. Agricultural Plastics

Agricultural plastics, including greenhouse films, irrigation pipes, and mulch films, are tested for UV protection and overall durability in farming environments. We ensure these materials are long-lasting and able to withstand the rigors of agricultural use.

13. Furniture & Appliances

Testing plastics used in furniture and appliances—such as plastic chairs, tables, and kitchen devices—ensures that these products are lightweight, aesthetically pleasing, and impact-resistant. We help ensure that your designs meet functional and safety standards.

14. Polymers for Coatings & Paints

Polymer-based paints, coatings, and varnishes are tested for properties like surface protection, corrosion resistance, and waterproofing. These materials are critical for providing long-lasting finishes on various surfaces, ensuring durability and protection from the elements.
