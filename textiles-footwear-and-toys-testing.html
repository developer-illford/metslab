What Standards Do We Follow?

We maintain strict compliance with globally recognized testing standards to ensure superior product quality and safety:

Textiles Standards
  • ISO 105 - Comprehensive colorfastness testing to washing, rubbing, and environmental factors
  • ISO 12947 - Advanced abrasion resistance testing using the Martindale method
  • OEKO-TEX Standard 100 - Ensuring textiles are completely free from harmful substances
  • ASTM D5034 - Sophisticated tensile strength and fabric durability testing
  • REACH (EU Regulation) - Full compliance with European chemical safety regulations
  • BS EN ISO 13934 - Textile tensile properties testing
  • BS EN ISO 12945 - Fabric pilling resistance assessment
Footwear Standards
  • SATRA TM144 - Advanced slip resistance testing methodologies
  • ISO 17707 - Comprehensive flex resistance testing of soles
  • ISO 16187 - Detailed analysis of antibacterial properties in footwear
  • ASTM D1052 - Thorough sole adhesion strength assessment
  • EN ISO 20344 - Complete performance testing for safety footwear
  • BS EN ISO 17708 - Upper sole bond strength testing
  • BS EN ISO 20871 - Abrasion resistance of soles
Toys Standards
  • EN 71 (European Toy Safety Directive) - Complete safety testing covering mechanical, chemical, and flammability hazards
  • ASTM F963 (USA) - Comprehensive toy safety standard implementation
  • ISO 8124 - Thorough international standard compliance for toy testing
  • CPSIA (Consumer Product Safety Improvement Act - USA) - Full compliance with child safety regulations
  • BS EN 62115 - Electric toys safety requirements
  • BS EN 71-13 - Olfactory board game requirements
