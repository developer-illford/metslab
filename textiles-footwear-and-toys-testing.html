Textile Labelling Regulations in the United Kingdom

The Textile Products (Labelling and Fibre Composition) Regulations require labelling for textile products in the United Kingdom. The regulations refer to the European Union Regulation (EU) 1007/2011, which requires fiber composition information for textile products. The regulation EU 1007/2011 covers textile products, including

  • Fibre composition: Products containing a minimum of 80% by weight of textile fibers. Furniture, sunshade coverings, and umbrellas with at least 80% textile content by weight. Textiles integrated into a product must have their fiber composition specified
  • Manufacturer’s information
  • Washing instruction
  • Retailer’s information
  • Size
  • Country of origin

The regulation EU 1007/2011 is fully adopted in France, Italy, Germany & Spain. Textile testing in the UK ensures compliance with these regulations
