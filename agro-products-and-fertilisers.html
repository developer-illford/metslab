What standards and regulations do we follow?

The Agro Products & Fertiliser Testing Services at our facility follow the complete set of international standards and European benchmarks which are considered the most demanding worldwide. Our facilities operate under UKAS (United Kingdom Accreditation Service) accreditation to conduct tests which equally follow the Fertilisers Manual (RB209) set by DEFRA for meeting strict UK agricultural regulations. The measurements performed by our laboratories comply with BS EN ISO/IEC 17025:2017 standards to maintain technical excellence and accuracy of all generated results.

We follow European standards beyond UK frameworks by implementing complete European testing procedures that follow the EU Fertilising Products Regulation 2019/1009 along with the COMAH and Code of Good Agricultural Practice for England and Wales regulations. The testing system we use examines agricultural chemical storage situations according to COMAH standards and water, soil and air protection criteria from the Code of Good Agricultural Practice for England and Wales. The multiple standardized standards guarantee agricultural items will comply with performance requirements and safety requirements as they move through global markets.

Our organization maintains dedicated membership with the European Committee for Standardization (CEN) technical committees that focus on fertilizers and soil improvers which helps us lead the field of developing modern testing methodologies. Our organization remains actively involved with both the Agricultural Industries Confederation (AIC) and the Environment Agency to maintain testing protocols that adhere to present environmental protection standards. Our comprehensive framework enables us to operate as a Certified Agro Products Testing Laboratory which provides testing services that surpass current regulations while predicting potential agricultural sector future regulations.

Comprehensive Agro Products & Fertilizer Testing:
1. Fertilizers

The effectiveness of fertilizers is critical to the growth and development of crops. We test a wide range of fertilizers to assess their nutrient content, purity, and efficiency in providing plants with the necessary elements for robust growth and high yields.

  • Nitrogen-Based Fertilizers: Urea and Ammonium Nitrate for rapid plant growth and soil fertility.
  • Phosphate-Based Fertilizers: Diammonium Phosphate (DAP) and Superphosphate for root development and long-term soil health.
  • Potassium-Based Fertilizers: Potassium Chloride and Potassium Sulfate for plant health and resistance to environmental stress.
  • Compound & Mixed Fertilizers: NPK fertilizers tailored to specific crop requirements.
  • Slow-Release & Controlled-Release Fertilizers: Designed for consistent nutrient supply and improved soil conditions.
  • Organic Fertilizers: Manure, compost, and plant-based fertilizers for sustainable soil enrichment.
  • Micronutrient Fertilizers: Zinc and Boron-enriched products for plant metabolism and development.
  • Liquid Fertilizers: Foliar sprays and soil enhancers for rapid nutrient absorption and soil health improvement.
2. Agricultural Products

The quality of agricultural products has a significant impact on the farming industry, and proper testing ensures that crops and other agricultural products meet the desired standards. We test various agricultural products to evaluate their quality, safety, and suitability for commercial use.

  • Grains: Wheat, rice, corn, and barley tested for contaminants, pests, diseases, moisture content, and protein levels.
  • Seeds: Crop seeds, vegetable seeds, and hybrid seeds evaluated for purity, germination rates, and disease resistance.
  • Pulses & Legumes: Lentils, chickpeas, and beans tested for nutritional standards and contaminants.
  • Fruits & Vegetables: Fresh and packaged produce tested for pesticide residues, microbial contamination, and ripeness.
  • Cash Crops: Cotton, tobacco, sugarcane, and coffee beans tested for quality benchmarks and economic viability.
  • Animal Feed: Polymer-packaged supplements and grains tested for nutritional balance and safety.
  • Organic Agricultural Inputs: Compost, biofertilisers, and bio-stimulants tested for sustainable farming practices.
