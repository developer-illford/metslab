Mandatory Labeling Requirements for Cosmetics Sold in the UK?

The following information must appear on the labels of cosmetic products;

  • The name and address of the designated responsible individual located in the United Kingdom.
  • Quantity declaration
  • The document requires the designation of a responsible individual for the UK. This person must provide their name and UK address.
  • Warnings and precautions
  • The batch numbers
  • The function of the product
  • The declaration of ingredients
  • The country of origin for imported products
